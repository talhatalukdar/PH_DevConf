FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register. Can't find your answer? Reach out to our team.

What is included in my conference ticket?

Every ticket includes full access to keynote sessions, workshops, networking events, and on-demand session recordings. Pro and Team tiers unlock additional perks such as VIP lounge access and 1:1 mentorship slots.

Is there a virtual attendance option?

Yes. All ticket tiers include a livestream pass with real-time Q&A. In-person attendance unlocks exclusive hands-on labs and offline networking sessions not available virtually.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Within 30 days, tickets can be transferred to a colleague or converted into credit for next year's conference.

Will session recordings be available after the event?

Absolutely. All attendees get lifetime access to recorded sessions, speaker slide decks, and workshop materials through our post-event content portal.

Do you offer group or student discounts?

Yes, we offer a 20% discount for groups of five or more and a 40% discount for verified students. Contact our support team with valid credentials to redeem your discount code.

What safety measures are in place at the venue?

The venue follows enhanced health and safety protocols including on-site medical staff, sanitization stations, and optional mask-friendly zones throughout the conference halls.
